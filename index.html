About Malka

Malka Martz is a comedy writer, director and producer, she is a 2024 Whistler Film Festival Lab participant and her work has travelled to various film festivals including Whistler Film Festival, Hollywood Comedy Shorts Festival, Toronto International Women’s Film Festival, the Santa Barbara Jewish Film Festival and LA Indie Student Film Festival. Malka has a BFA in film from the Capilano University. Malka's plan is to transform the world into a more just place through comedy and explore the new ways humour can be used to grapple with tough subjects. Her work is driven by curiosity and out-of the box thinking and a passion to talk about herself in the third person.
