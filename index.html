About Malka

Malka Martz is an independent comedy writer, director and producer based in Vancouver, Canada. Malka has a BFA from the Capilano University School Of Motion Picture Arts. Her work has travelled to various film festivals including the Whistler Film Festival, Toronto International Women’s Film Festival, the 8th annual Hollywood Comedy Shorts Film Festival, Los Angeles Student Film Festival, and the Santa Barbara Jewish Film Festival. Malka's plan is to transform the world into a more just place through comedy and explore the new ways humour can be used to grapple with tough subjects. Her work is driven by curiosity and out-of the box thinking and a passion to talk in third person.
